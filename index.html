I am a CS PhD student at UC San Diego where I am extremely fortunate to be advised by Prof. Julian McAuley. My (current) research interests in Machine Learning are Data-centric AI (a nice definition by Prof. Andrew Ng), Recommender Systems, Large-scale ML, and Bandits (in no particular order).

More specifically, I have been exploring problems related to data distillation (WSDM '22, arXiv '22), offline RL (KDD '20), extreme classification (WWW '21), and personalization (RecSys '18, ECIR '18, WSDM '19, SIGIR '20).
